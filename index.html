About PREM EDUCATION

PREM EDUCATION is a social initiative started to support poor and underprivileged children by providing them with free education. The aim of this program is to ensure that no child is deprived of learning because of financial difficulties.

Each child also receives ₹500 every month to help buy books, notebooks, and learning resources.
